Journal of Genetics

 

Journal of Genetics (ISSN:0022-1333), published by
Indian Academy of Sciences since 1985 (see the short note on the journal's history), covers all areas of genetics and evolution, including molecular genetics and molecular evolution. Three issues are published each year in one volume, in April, August and December.

The journal accepts submissions only via the Web, at www.editorialmanager.com/jgen/. Please read the 'Information for authors' document. The brief note titled 'Academy policy on plagiarism' (PDF file) spells out how the publisher of the journal, Indian Academy of Sciences, will handle cases of plagiarism in the journal detected by the editorial team or brought to their notice by reviewers or others before or after publication.
