journal of physics

Journal of Physics is a monthly research journal in English published by the Indian Academy of Sciences in collaboration with Indian National Science Academy and Indian Physics Association. The journal publishes refereed papers covering current research in Physics, both original contributions - research papers, brief reports or rapid communications - and invited reviews. Pramana also publishes special issues devoted to advances in specific areas of Physics and proceedings of select high quality conferences.

  • Covered by leading international indexing and abstracting services
  • Twelve monthly issues and over 2500 pages published annually
  • Papers generally published within six months of submission, and within three months of final acceptance
  • Rigorous scrutiny of articles by referees selected from a comprehensive referee database
  • Style file for typesetting from LaTeX available at this site
  • Faster processing with electronic submission
  • (preferably as LaTeX files and figures as EPS files)
  • 50 reprints of articles supplied free
  • No page charges
  • Colour reproduction of figures at no extra cost for webpages. For print version actual charges to be borne by authors
