Information about nomination to the fellowship

  1. Nomination forms are provided only to the Fellows and are not made available on the Academy website.
  2. Those pursuing research in India are eligible to be nominated.
  3. The last date for receipt of new nominations is 31st May.
  4. The Academy offers only fellowship and not membership against payment.

The process of election of Fellows is described in the statutes.
