Indian Academy of Sciences The Indian Academy has been active in promoting science education in India. For details see the webpage. These pages have been put together to help students of chemistry at various universities in India, studying at the Bachelor's level.  These pages contain links to the study materials already available in the web on topics related to Organic, Inorganic and Physical Chemistry, at a level appropriate to the students of Bachelor's degree courses in Indian Universities.
